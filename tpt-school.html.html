About Us

TPT is a platform by Mexicans for Mexicans to alleviate the level of poverty and suffering in our society — a platform where we all help each other and stand in the gap between Mexicans and other Mexicans. Since the government has failed to be of help and assistance to the people as they should, we are not a Ponzi, pyramid, or get-rich-quick scheme, or even a platform where people get robbed of their hard-earned resources.

Ideology

The modern world is bad — it is inhumane, unfair, and unjust. It is a world of money. It is not made for people. It is made for those who make money — the rich, bankers, politicians, and the government. Ordinary people are servants for the rich, sweeping out their palaces.

Many people live on the breadline, unable to maintain themselves or their families, buy clothes, or afford material values. TPT creates an alternative to the current financial system based on equality, sincerity, transparency, and matching rewards for every effort.

What is Money?

Money is the “shackles” into which we are put by banks and the government. We can do nothing without money — buy food, manage a disease, etc. In truth, it is just pieces of paper backed by nothing. The modern financial system keeps people selling their time and skills for money — which is never enough. TPT aims to reduce dependence on that system by enabling community-based aid.

About the Founder

James Andrade McAllister is the founder and leader of the TPT Community — a lifetime project aimed at defying the unjust financial system worldwide. He stands for financial freedom and believes happiness doesn’t require much money, only the ability to use it freely.

McAllister is an economist, statistician, and large-scale farmer from the USA who lived in Vietnam for many years. He started TPT with friends and raised funds to help the needy. Over 11,000 people benefitted from his gestures in Dak Lak before TPT expanded internationally.

History of Creation

2016: TPT was founded in Vietnam by James Andrade McAllister and friends.
2016–2019: Rapid growth and community experiments took place; thousands were assisted locally.
2019–2024: Online launch, international expansion, added Bitcoin transfers, charity events, and claimed millions of participants.
Present: TPT positions itself as a voluntary community focused on mutual help and legal compliance.

FAQ

Quick answers to common questions.

Is this an investment?

No — contributions are gifts to meet verified needs. There are no guaranteed returns.

How are requests verified?

Applicants submit documents; reviewers validate details according to public criteria.

Are there fees?

Only payment processor fees and small compliance costs. Full breakdowns are published in reports.

Can I choose who I support?

Yes — you can support specific requests or donate to pooled categories like medical or education funds.
