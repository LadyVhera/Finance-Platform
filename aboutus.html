About Us

TPT is a platform by Mexicans for Mexicans to alleviate the level of poverty and suffering in our society — a platform where we all help each other and stand in the gap between Mexicans and other Mexicans. Since the government has failed to be of help and assistance to the people as they should, we are not a Ponzi, pyramid, or get-rich-quick scheme, or even a platform where people get robbed of their hard-earned resources.

Ideology

The modern world is bad — it is inhumane, unfair, and unjust. It is a world of money. It is not made for people. It is made for those who make money — the rich, bankers, politicians, and the government. Ordinary people are servants for the rich, sweeping out their palaces.

Many people live on the breadline, unable to maintain themselves or their families, buy clothes, or afford material values. TPT creates an alternative to the current financial system based on equality, sincerity, transparency, and matching rewards for every effort.

What is Money?

Money is "shackles" into which we are put by banks and the government. You can do nothing without money: neither buy food, nor manage a disease. We got used to the fact that everything is done for money, and we highly appreciate It. But in fact, it is just pieces of paper printed by banks, with people's signatures, which are backed by nothing. These pieces of paper are given to people as payment for their work. The US Fed has no external constraints and can print dollars in unlimited quantities. Central banks of other countries are regulated by the Fed. Theoretically, money can be printed in the amount as large as one wishes so that will suffice for everyone. But the economic system is structured in such a way that we sell our time, skills and knowledge for money, which is never enough.

History of Creation

2016: TPT was first founded by James Andrade McAllister back in 2016 in company of good meaning friends and business associate, in Vietnam.
2019: TPT Is started up on the Internet. It mainly includes citizens from Southern American countriés. James Andrade McAllister changed his role of the owner for the role of an "advisor- consultant". Every week he records video and voice messages to the Community members with tips and recommendations on participation. All funds are kept on the accounts of the participants themselves. Mr McAllister has no power over the funds.
2020: James Andrade McAllister finally creates equal positions for all participants. Now everyone receives the same percentage of the provided help amount, regardless of the number of attracted new members. However, participants do receive bonuses for each invited user.
2021: TTPT becomes very popular and goes beyond South American countries. Participants from all over the world start to join the Community. By the end of 2021 there are already 35 million participants in TPT.
2022: TPT continues to admit participants from different parts of the world Asia, Africa, Eastern Europe, Europe, America. The Community introduces the possibility using the Bitcoin cryptocurrency. Now people can freely transfer money to each other, even if they live in different states. At the end of 2022, the number of TPT participants amounts to 70 milllon.
2023: TPT starts to arrange charity events In different countries. TPT leaders give food to the poor, school supplies to children, and so on. By the end of the year, 150 millon people participates in TPT.
2024: TPT keeps growing worldwide. Participants continue to hold charity events, donate help to those in need. The growth rate ls constantly increasing. There are already more than 200 million participants from 118 countries in the Community! Soon the entire world will Join TPT!

What is FELICIDAD?

FELICIDAD is TPT's conditional currency unit. The amount of help provided Is displayed in TPT's conditional currency units -- FELICIDAD. For example, if a participant provides help for MXN 20,000, Felicidad- MXN 20,000 is accrued on their account. Felicidas grows at a monthly rate of 30% and can be exchanged for real money at any time. A certain percent is added to your amount in Felicidad on a daily basis.
