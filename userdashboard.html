Policies & Safeguards

TPT is a voluntary mutual-aid community — not an investment product. Contributions are gifts to meet verified needs. No guaranteed returns.

TPT brings dignity-first help in a world where many feel left behind. We aim to build an alternative rooted in equality, sincerity, transparency, and matching recognition to real effort — so people can pursue education, business, travel, and family life in abundance.

From the shared materials: money can become “shackles” when access is scarce. TPT doesn’t sell money or returns — we coordinate mutual aid so members can solve urgent, real problems quickly and accountably.

  • 2016: Initial community effort started abroad; later reorganized.
  • 2019–2024: Online relaunch; cross-region participation and community events; emphasis on transparency.
Narrative provided by the community; verify independently before external citation.

Any mentions of FELICIDAD or “bonuses” are community-provided, illustrative ideas — not guarantees, not investment returns, and not promises.
  • FELICIDAD: an internal recognition unit historically mentioned to record contributions. Treat as symbolic, not a currency.
  • Registration/Testimonial/Referral recognitions: framed as trust-building gestures with strict caps and transparency, never pay-to-recruit.

  • Adults only.
  • Provide pledged help within 72 hours.
  • No multiple accounts or shared details.
  • Family referrals follow published caps.
  • Privacy-safe proofs; consent before publishing stories.

The community is stable when members act in good faith: verify needs, avoid over-promising, diversify donations across categories, and invite responsibly.

Participation carries risk. Contributions are gifts, not investments; there are no guaranteed returns. Always follow your country’s laws.
