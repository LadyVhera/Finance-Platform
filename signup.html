About TPT

TPT is a platform built by Mexicans for Mexicans to reduce immediate suffering and create pathways to stability. We connect people who need help with community members who can provide it — with public rules, careful verification, and clear reporting.

We are not an investment program, a pyramid scheme, or an automatic income; we are a community of people helping people — honestly and transparently.

View governance & safeguards
Founder — James Andrade McAllister

An economist and farmer focused on practical ways to lift people from scarcity. James helped thousands in early projects before expanding TPT internationally. His mission: make help fast, legal and accountable.

FAQ

Quick answers to common questions.

Is this an investment?

No — contributions are gifts to meet verified needs. There are no guaranteed returns.

How are requests verified?

Applicants submit documents; reviewers validate details according to public criteria.

Are there fees?

Only payment processor fees and small compliance costs. Full breakdowns are published in reports.

Can I choose who I support?

Yes — you can support specific requests or donate to pooled categories like medical or education funds.
